Introduction

        Pollution is the introduction of harmful substances or contaminants into the environment, causing adverse effects on ecosystems, human health, and well-being. It can take various forms, such as air pollution, water pollution, soil contamination, and noise pollution. The sources of pollution are diverse, including industrial activities, transportation, agriculture, and improper waste disposal. Addressing pollution is crucial to safeguarding the planet and ensuring a sustainable future for all living beings.
